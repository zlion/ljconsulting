About Us

What we do

Experts in college admissions dedicated to guiding high school students through the application process and helping them gain acceptance to their target universities.

LJ Consulting specializes in step-by-step high school planning, mentoring research projects, and guiding students through application essays and scholarship submissions. We provide tailored solutions designed to support students aiming for top universities, leveraging our insights and expertise.

As a guiding principle, our goal is not to dominate our industry but to excel in the specific niche we serve.
